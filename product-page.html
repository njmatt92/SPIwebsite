Description

MAC Equipment Co. model 96AVR52 round pulse jet dust collector for heavy dust loads. This unit has 690 sq.ft. of bag surface area for 6091 cfm. The AVR series is designed for vacuum, pressure and combination vacuum/pressure bulk material handling and dust collection systems.
